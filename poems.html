a poem

everything i know has come to an end when will i begin again
another life
another chosen fallen
another baby bird
another swallow
and mother tells me this is it
may as well enjoy it
and mother shows me this is all
i am everything
and everything is one
and everything is all
but i'm still twisting in my darkness
still turning in my grave
still remembering lost sorrows
and forgiving all i gave
i've seen mountains never breathed upon
a beauty unsurpassed
but i never felt the chill of nightfall
internal beauty always lasts
so i sit in the familiar
we play these twisted games
we laugh when it starts snowing
after days and days of rain
the leaves of autumn cover me
slowly rot and turn to cold
the sun in spring recovers me
and i relearn the things i know
nothing can take this away
the way i feel on a sunny day
no nothing can take this away
and i don't have to forget
never forget the things you left
never forget to rewind
rewind sometimes...

ghouls

i feel the pull it calls me
as these walls rise up inside me
i hear screaming let it free
i feel it breathing in

i inhale my skin turns pale
i try to rid these thoughts within
the blade sits close to the demons throat
always threatening

need to find an inspiration
inside i think i'm drowning
drying up, getting sick
trying to slow down my dying

need to stop this wasting
every day no soul no tasting
push on through, give in to you
put on a mask to the ghouls i'm facing

i want to climb mountains so high there is no oxygen
swim so deep i see only darkness
lose my mind and lose my mind
get lost in time

where the only color i see is red green blue
yellow orange and a shade of you
a minute has passed and i swear i knew
a secret only shared to few


- infusca
